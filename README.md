# ECE 5020: Digital Signal Processing

**ECE Course — M.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering**
Tennessee Technological University | Spring 2026

## Course Description
Theory and practice of discrete-time signals and systems, A/D and D/A conversion, filter design,
DSP architecture and implementation, programming, and DSP applications.

## About
Digital Signal Processing systems are the enabling technology for innovations spanning smartphones,
medical equipment, aerospace, communications, and robotics. ECE 5020 introduces the fundamental
concepts and tools used in the analysis and design of commonly employed DSP systems, bridging
rigorous mathematical foundations in signals and systems theory with real-world applications.
